## Understanding Slot RTP

RTP (Return to Player) is the percentage of wagered money a slot returns to players over time. A 96% RTP means the slot returns $96 for every $100 wagered on average. Higher RTP slots offer better long-term odds.

### RTP Guidelines

- **96%+** — Good RTP, recommended
- **94-96%** — Average, acceptable
- **Below 94%** — Below average, consider alternatives

## Volatility Explained

### Low Volatility

## Are Crypto Slots Provably Fair?

Some crypto casinos offer provably fair slots where you can verify each spin's fairness using blockchain technology. These systems use cryptographic hash functions that allow players to mathematically verify outcomes weren't predetermined or manipulated.

However, most slots from major providers like NetEnt, Pragmatic Play, and Hacksaw Gaming use traditional RNG systems verified by third-party auditors rather than provably fair mechanisms.
